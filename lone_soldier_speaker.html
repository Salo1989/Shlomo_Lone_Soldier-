Shlomo Elguera Reservist and Kiryat Arba Resident

About

Shlomo was born in Miami, Florida in 1989 and grew up in Broward county , Florida. Aftering studying in both Florida Atlantic University and Yeshiva University he immigratted to Israel in 2012. After serving in the IDF for two years as a combat soldier he went to learn in Yeshivat Shavie Hebron. Shlomo Currently is a reservist, a blogger for the Times of Israel and has experience working security under the Ministry of Education for three years. He Currently lives in Kiryat Arba, Israel.

Military Service

Shlomo was drafted into the Kfir brigrade in the IDF and served as a combat soldier under the MOS of a heavy machine gunner. Shlomo took part in counter terror operations, war games and operating at a forward operating base. He served in Samaria areas of Tulkarem, Jennin and Ramallah.

After the Military

After the military Shlomo moved to Hebron where he studied in Yeshivat Shavie Hebron where he learned Talmud, and Jewish scriptures. Afterwards he moved to Kiryat Arba where he worked at Midreshet Hebron as a sercurity guard for tours. In 2016 he volunteered for reservist on Duty were he flew to Los Angeles and spoke about his experience in the IDF.
